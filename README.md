# Dr. Hakikur Rahman

Professor | Researcher | Editor | ICT4D Specialist

## About Me

Dr. Hakikur Rahman is a distinguished academic, researcher, and editor with over four decades of experience in higher education, research, and international development across Bangladesh and abroad.

He currently serves as Professor and Head of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the International Standard University (ISU), Bangladesh, and Director of the Sustainability Research Institute at Ansted University, Malaysia.

His research interests include Artificial Intelligence, ICT for Development (ICT4D), Knowledge Management, Digital Governance, Open Innovation, Data Mining, Computer Education, and E-Government.

He currently serves as Editor-in-Chief of:
- International Journal of ICT and Human Development (IJICTHD)
- International Journal of Knowledge Management (IJKM)
